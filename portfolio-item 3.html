Healthy Response

We ALL get triggered. We ALL have coping mechanisms. Healthy Response, by VanGUARD Health Solutions, aims to be your go-to resource during emotional distress. The customizable, mobile-friendly application was created to help you counteract life's most challenging circumstances with positive reactions. Built with in-app user messaging, a personalized assessment, and a multitude of relevant resources, Healthy Response sets out to destigmatize mental health issues and benefit users with applicable information and a community of support.
